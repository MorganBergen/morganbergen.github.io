What is consulting?

Consulting is the process of supporting clients to reach their goals through advising, implementing, and operating effective solutions to large scale business processes.

What about consulting?

- Consultants have a natural curiosity for solving problems
- Are comfortable with ambiguity
- Help clients achieve their stategic goals and solve complex problems through innovative solutions
- Brings learning agility to work across multiple industries and multiple business challenges.

Responsibilities vary, however some of the roles you may have as an analyst are:
- Research best business practices
- Perform financial and competitive analyses
- Solve problems and provide creative insight
- Conduct client interviews
- Desin systems and processes
- Participate in and lead presentations to clients
- Plan and execute knowledge transfer sessions with clients
